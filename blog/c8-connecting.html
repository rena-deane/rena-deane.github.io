Empathy, Trust, Kindness

Growing Trust

For building trust I found the following tips doable.

  1. Practise giving people the benefit of the doubt - most people do what they do because it feels like the right thing at the time. It makes sense to them.
  2. Remember that trust begets trust - to build trust with you I have to assume you are trustworthy and to treat you that way. When you feel tjat someone trusts you, it makes it easier to trust them back. Vive versa!
Empathetic Listening

I can't say I practiced this formally but a few years ago when I was speaking to someone about a difficult situation they were in I made sure I heard correctly by giving my version of what they said. I wanted to know how this person felt then I suggested ways to seek a resolution. I was also empathetic because I had been in that situation before and the similarities between this person and myself were uncanny.

Loving Kindness Meditation

This practice is very easy. Wishing kindness on another is very generous and changes your attitude towards that person especially if they're not your favourite.

Political Awareness

I can't think of a situation where I haven't taken someone elses perspective into consideration. However I have to wait a while to accept someone elses perspective on the matter. Ignorance is the hardest thing to accept and this is where I choose which battles to fight. Although I might not agree with what someone says I can understand how they think that way. Everything happens for a reason right?!
