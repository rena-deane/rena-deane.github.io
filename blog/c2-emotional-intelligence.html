Search Inside Yourself

Saturday 26 March 2016

Chapter 1

Reading and watching chapter 1 makes me want to learn about improving my emotional competencies. Being aware that these exist make it more realistic to achieve the state of being. I find myself either thinking about the past and future and forget live in the present. Through mindfulness it's exciting to know I can train myself to switch into a state of calmness and clarity on demand. It sounds simple and almost too good to be true. I think the challenge is to keep practising. The habit I created for myself is similar to the 2 minutes of mindfulness. I challenged myself to sit for 2 minutes with my eyes closed, silent, before I started a waffle ticket. I think I should tweek it to be 2 minutes before every meal. My silent prayer.

Another interesting lesson was the one about attention. Recognising what your body is trying to tell you without judgement.

I found the two minutes of mindfulness very soothing and relaxing. I found myself switching between the Easy Way and the Easier Way. The two minutes went by very fast and it gave me a little energy burst. I think it has a similar effect to the power nap.

I'm very keen on this book and its teachings and excited about the positive effects it will have on me. I look forward to reading the rest of the book.
