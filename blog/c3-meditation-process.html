Redesign and Meditation

Process over Product

Before I started the rebuilding exercise I told myself I was going to timebox to help me stay focussed. That quickly went out the window as I explored new ways of doing things. Everytime I went off and searched for solutions I pulled myself back and told myself that the site doesn't have to be perfect. Which is why it doesn't match the wireframe.

I found that with each element that wasn't doing what I intended, I learnt more about. Google searching, looking at other sites and using the Chrome Dev Tools helped me to understand how to structure code, the importance of naming classes, and layouts.

Search Inside Yourself - Meditation

Before reading chapter 2 I thought meditation was the practice of settling the mind. That you sit and focus on your breath and that if your mind wonders your not really meditating. I also believed that you weren't to open your eyes otherwise you had to start again.

When reading this chapter I learnt about Meta-Attention, attention to your attention. Something like that. More fascinating to me was the comparison to exercise. Specifically how to deal with distractions when you meditate. Everytime your mind is distracted bring your focus back to your breathing. When you do this you are training muscels in the brain to focus your attention. So it's not a bad thing to let your mind wonder as long as you recognise it and bring your focus back to your breathing.

I like the part where it says that our bodies aren't use to traditional postures so we can find the one that suits us. I took that as a small challenge so I'm going to sit on the floor, legs crossed but with a back rest.

When I practiced 10 minutes of meditation there we alot of disctractions. A fly on my face, itchy head, I needed to click my toes (it's a disgusting habit of mine). Instead of reacting as soon as I felt these sensations I took five breaths then attended to it. Good muscle building exercise for the brain
